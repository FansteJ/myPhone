What is a calculator?

Calculator is an app used to perform calculations, ranging from basic arithmetic to complex mathematics. there are scientific calculators, which include trigonometric and statistical calculations. Some calculators even have the ability to do computer algebra. Graphing calculators can be used to graph functions defined on the real line, or higher-dimensional Euclidean space.
